# YurrMom Non-Negotiables

These principles govern product, design, architecture, monetization, moderation, and roadmap decisions.

## 1. The mission is transferred experience

YurrMom exists to reduce the amount of practical household knowledge each person must reproduce alone.

The platform should help answer questions such as:

- What am I doing?
- How do I organize my family’s life?
- What is for dinner?
- What should I buy, and how should I use it?
- How does someone in circumstances like mine make this work?

The enduring asset is organized lived experience—not a feed of affiliate links.

## 2. Systems before products

A product link without context has little durable value.

A creator should be able to explain:

- the problem being solved
- the household circumstances
- the complete list or workflow
- how the items work together
- timing, frequency, substitutions, and tradeoffs
- what has changed through experience

Products belong inside lists and systems. Systems do not exist merely to hold products.

## 3. Creators are partners, not inventory

Creators keep ownership of their:

- identity and brand
- audience relationships
- content
- affiliate accounts and earnings
- sponsorships
- external publishing channels

YurrMom should not take a commission on creator affiliate earnings. Optional services may eventually be offered transparently, but the platform’s core promise must remain intact.

## 4. Create once; publish everywhere

The creator interface should make YurrMom the preferred place to originate structured household content because one source can produce many outputs.

A creator may begin with one household story, list, routine, or system. YurrMom should help prepare appropriate expressions for:

- the creator’s YurrMom profile
- TikTok
- Instagram
- YouTube Shorts
- Pinterest
- Facebook
- newsletters
- blogs or personal websites
- affiliate storefronts and link destinations

The canonical knowledge object is more important than any single social post.

## 5. Portable lists

A household list must not be owned by a retailer.

The platform should preserve the creator’s intent while allowing a user to act through available services such as grocery delivery, personal shopping, retailer carts, affiliate storefronts, printable lists, exports, or manual shopping.

Retailers and delivery providers are replaceable integrations around a stable YurrMom list model.

## 6. Connect existing infrastructure; do not rebuild it

YurrMom was conceived before modern grocery delivery and personal-shopping infrastructure became widespread. That infrastructure now exists.

YurrMom should organize knowledge and orchestrate handoffs—not attempt to become a grocery retailer, delivery fleet, universal checkout, or warehouse system unless future evidence strongly justifies it.

## 7. Entertainment is the doorway

Humor can attract people who were not actively searching for household guidance.

The roast section, funny advertisements, memes, and shareable entertainment are acquisition and community mechanisms. They should lead visitors toward creators, lists, systems, merchandise, and useful household knowledge.

Entertainment must not become the complete identity of the platform.

## 8. Fictional targets for roast content

Roast prompts should default to clearly fictional, synthetic, or AI-generated adults and recognizable household archetypes rather than identifiable private people.

Humor should focus on behavior, situations, choices, and cultural archetypes—not protected traits, children, doxxing, or coordinated harassment.

## 9. YurrMom merchandise funds YurrMom

Original YurrMom merchandise fulfilled through Printful is the primary native monetization path currently envisioned.

This allows creator distribution and cross-promotion to remain generous while YurrMom earns from its own creative work and brand equity.

Commerce should support the mission without displacing it.

## 10. Flexibility over platform dependency

Do not hard-code the business around one:

- retailer
- affiliate network
- social network
- fulfillment provider
- AI model or provider
- authentication vendor
- payment service

Use stable internal abstractions and adapters where integration is required.

## 11. Structured knowledge over disposable posts

Every important creator contribution should strengthen a reusable knowledge structure.

Videos, captions, images, and posts are expressions of underlying objects such as:

- household systems
- lists
- routines
- recipes
- products
- substitutions
- stories
- constraints
- creator experience

The platform should preserve the relationship between those objects.

## 12. Build the narrowest coherent foundation

The complete vision is broad. Fable 5 should not attempt to implement every integration and future capability in one pass.

The first build should establish a coherent foundation that visibly expresses the full product thesis while using honest placeholders or adapters for capabilities that require external approval, APIs, authentication, or later operational work.

A smaller truthful system is preferable to a polished interface that falsely implies unsupported automation.
